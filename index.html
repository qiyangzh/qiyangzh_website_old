About Me

I am an incoming PhD student in Education at Johns Hopkins University. I am currently an M.S.Ed. student in Education Policy at the Graduate School of Education, University of Pennsylvania, and expect to graduate in August. Before coming to Penn, I graduated from the National University of Singapore with Bachelors degrees in Economics (with Honors and Distinction) and Industrial Systems Engineering.

I am learning how to apply quantitative research skills to educational research and policy design. My passion is to inform, inspire, and educate. In the future, I look forward to teaching at a university.
